Import Yelp Reviews

Since Yelp blocks automated scraping, you can extract your own reviews by running a script while you're logged in. Follow the steps below:

1 Drag the button to your bookmarks bar

Drag the button below into your browser's bookmarks bar. (Show bookmarks bar: ⌘⇧B on Mac)

⭐ Extract Yelp Reviews

2 Go to your Yelp reviews page

Visit: https://www.yelp.com/user_details_reviews_self?userid=FKPUeSBvXP5vde2FSJOGow

3 Click the bookmarklet

Click "Extract Yelp Reviews" in your bookmarks bar. A JSON file will download automatically.

4 Upload the JSON here

Click to select yelp-reviews.json
